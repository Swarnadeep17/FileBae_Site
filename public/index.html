What is FileBae?

FileBae is a minimalist file conversion platform that helps you perform everyday tasks like turning PDFs into images, converting spreadsheets to PDFs, or extracting content from photos — all in one place. Designed to be fast and distraction-free, it doesn't ask for your email or throw ads in your face. Just upload and convert.

Why FileBae Stands Out

While many platforms gatekeep basic tools or bury them behind registration walls, FileBae does the opposite. With no ads, logins, or paywalls, our focus is simplicity. Tools are powered by lightweight cloud services and open-source intelligence, ensuring fast results across devices.

Use Cases You Didn’t Know You Needed

Use FileBae to submit assignment images as PDFs, extract legal documents into text using OCR, compress resumes for job applications, or batch convert receipts into spreadsheets. We build tools for real-world usage.

Optimized for Every Device

Whether you're on mobile or desktop, FileBae adapts seamlessly. There's no need to install apps or software — everything runs straight in the browser, secured and encrypted. You retain control of your files at all times.
